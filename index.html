Promoting the music of Rabindranath Tagore

Swaralipi is a music academy based in Wayland, Massachusetts run by Swapna Ray. In this academy, children of all ages and adults learn Rabindrasangeet, a genre of Indian semi-classical music, and its classical basis. Swapna is a disciple of late Suchitra Mitra, a doyen of Rabindranath's music. The goal of Swaralipi is to spread Rabindrasangeet among Bengalis, non-Bengalis and non-Indians.

Meet our singing teacher!

Swapna Ray

Ms.Swapna Ray (lovingly called Swapna Mashi by her students) is a direct disciple of late Ms.Suchitra Mitra, a doyen of in the Rabindrasangeet genre. Swapna embraced the unique gayaki of Ms.Mitra and has been passing it along to her students in the style of Guru-Shishya Parampara through her music school Swaralipi Academy of Music in Wayland, MA. Swaralipi was founded by Swapna in 2007, and since its inception nearly one hundred children, young adults, and adults have been trained by Swapna. Swapna has presented Rabindrasangeet and other music in numerous stages in Kolkata and North America.
